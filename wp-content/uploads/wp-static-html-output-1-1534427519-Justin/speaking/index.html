Justin Jackson speaking bio:

Justin Jackson is the founder of MegaMaker, where he advises software, SaaS, and digital product companies. He’s also the author of Marketing for Developers and Jolt, which have sold 6,000+ copies. His blog, podcasts, and videos focus on topics like growth, marketing, landing pages, and product development.
