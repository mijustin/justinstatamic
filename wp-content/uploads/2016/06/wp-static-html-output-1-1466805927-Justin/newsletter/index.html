Who am I?

Hi, I’m Justin Jackson.

I’m the former Product Manager at Sprint.ly, and have consulted on marketing & growth for startups in London, San Francisco, Boulder, and Portland.

In 2015 I decided I wanted to make my own stuff. I published his first book, Marketing for Developers. Currently, I’m working on MegaMaker and Remote Workers Club.
