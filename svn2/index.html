A great customer support experience

Why Litmus has good support:

I emailed Danielle, asking why email clients need to be selected to do spam testing. She got back to me right away. Her email was friendly, and to the point. She explained that "for the moment all email clients need to be selected in order to run the tests." Danielle apologized for how inefficient this was, and mentioned that they were currently looking for ways for users to choose individual tests.

What I liked most about the experience: I felt like I was talking to a human being. Her personality really came out in her response, which made it feel genuine and real.
