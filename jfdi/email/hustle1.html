Next Saturday, November 9th, I'll be opening registration for the Week of Hustle. This is a week-long email bootcamp just for JFDI members. Best of all: it's included in your monthly membership.

This 7 day bootcamp is designed to help you build a product in 1 week.

Every day, you'll get a challenge by email. You'll complete the challenge, and then report back (to keep you accountable).

The bootcamp is a perfect fit if you're:

  • Looking to build a small product in 1 week
  • Looking to finish a product you've alreay started, but are stagnating on.

After a week, you'll have worked hard, and shipped something! Life's too short to let any more weeks slip by.

Are you in?

Remember: this is going to require at least a few hours of work each day. Check your calendar: make sure you can seriously commit before signing up.

I'll send out the registration link early in the morning this coming Saturday (November 9th). Once you sign-up you'll receive your first email automatically. Each day, we'll discuss what we did on the Week of Hustle discussion group on JFDI.

Cheers!
Justin Jackson

PS: this bootcamp is for people who have already built an audience, and started to identify their pain points. If you haven't done that yet, stay tuned for the Build an Audience bootcamp.
