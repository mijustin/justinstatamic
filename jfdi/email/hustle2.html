Here's how it works:

  • Every day, you'll get a challenge by email.
  • You'll complete the challenge and then report back in the discussion forum.
  • Bonus: I'd also encourage you to write a daily blog, sharing your progress (see below) and collecting email addresses.

The idea is that after a week, you'll have worked hard, and shipped something! Life's too short to let any more weeks slip by.

Let's talk about blogging: JFDI member Andy Parkinson went from idea to paying customers in 7 days. He had a great launch, in part, because he was blogging every day, and sharing the product development process with the world. This meant that every day, he was a building an audience that he could launch to.

Nathan Barry just spoke about this in this interview as well: his approach is to announce that you're building a product, and allow people to sign-up to get updates. People that sign-up for a mailing list about a product have "intent": they are likely the kind of people that would buy your product when you launch.
