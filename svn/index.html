A great customer support experience

Litmus

Litmus is software that tests how an HTML email will render in different email clients. They recently added a feature that runs your email through popular spam filters, to see if there are any red flags.

I emailed their support team a simple question: "Why do all email clients need to be selected to do spam testing?"

What made it great:

Danielle from Litmus got back to me right away. Her email was friendly, and got right to the point. She explained that "for the moment all email clients need to be selected in order to run the tests." Danielle apologized for how inefficient this was, and mentioned that they were currently looking for ways for users to choose individual tests.

What I liked most about the experience: I felt like I was talking to a human being. Her personality really came out in her response, which made it feel genuine and real.
